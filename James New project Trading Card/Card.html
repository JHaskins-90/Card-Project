Monitor Lizard

Monitor lizards, known for their remarkable size, can grow up to 10 feet long, making them the largest lizards on our planet!

  • Scientific Name: Varanus
  • Diet: Mostly Carnivorous
  • Lifespan:8-30 years
  • Social Structure: Mostly Solitary

Within their native habitats spanning Africa, Asia, and Oceania, monitor lizards exhibit an impressive diversity, encompassing approximately 80 recognized species.
